About OC Manufacturing

O.C. Manufacturing was born in January-2003 as wholesaler/retailer of paper of any different types and/or requested applications. The merchandise is located beside the Paranaque Wet Market and caters mostly customers where application includes books, posters, calendars, off-set papers, etc.

It has established partnership with couple of international paper suppliers as accredited local dealer since then.

With the big difference between traditional merchandise and custom made paper, the company have decided to separate those entities. Wholesale/Retail stores most clients are schools and printing companies while custom paper for SUNPOWER requires very strict quality compliance.

From then on, interleaf paper manufacturing became autonomous entity that focuses on customized paper design. This gave the company also a focus on doing a semiconductor approach on quality and business process as required by the customer. Currently, for interleaf manufacturing, we now have Two (2) Machines worth P1.6M of capital. Two (2) manufacturing sites where each site has one operating machine. Total employees as of 2009 is at seventeen (17) people.

In July 2005 when our custom-made book-paper passed qualification in SUNPOWER It was a customize design fit for SUNPOWER application and manufactured locally by O.C. Manufacturing.

In 2010 we are introducing our new Division for our Manufacturing line of Rubber and Engineering Plastic Products.
